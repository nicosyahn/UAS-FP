DETAILS

  • PHASE ONE : Let's get this Party Started.

    GIVEAWAYS FOR OUR TOODLERS FAMILY

    During the first phase of our journey, we will be hosting various giveaways for our community. These giveaways will include anything from Toodlers Family List spots to physical items and more.


    SHOWING LOVE TO OUR REAL WORLD TOODLERS

    During this beginning phase, we will also be giving back to people in our real world community. We have started a TikTok where we will be posting videos of our team showing love to people in our community who need a helping hand, and various other good deeds.

    Our hashtag #TSTD will be used on every video in hopes of inspiring our growing community to take part in our movement.

  • PHASE TWO : Release the Toodlers - Mint.

    This is the part of our journey where the Toodlers come to life. Minting will happen in two stages, the pre-sale mint, and the public mint. Pre-sale will be open to OG's and Toodlerslist members only.


    SPECIAL ITEMS/PERKS FOR HOLDERS

    After the mint, holders of Toodlers will be eligible to receive special items and perks including exclusive airdrops, merchandise, and much more.


    GIVING BACK TO HOLDERS

    Holders will also be entered into large weekly, holder only giveaways. These giveaways will include physical items, crypto/NFT's, and much more. This is one of many ways holders will be given value for holding a Toodlers.

  • PHASE THREE : Enough Wandering Around.

    COMMUNITY EVENTS

    Events for holders will be held in the Toodlers metaverse. These events will include contests, games, and much more.

    In addition to metaverse events, we will also be doing real life events with select holders.


    TOODLERS FOR GOOD

    During this phase of the journey, we will also be continuing to make videos on our TikTok as well as doing various other charitable initiatives. Our community will have the opportunity to suggest and vote on what they would like to see us do, and what people/communities they feel are deserving of having their lives changed.


    TOODLERS FAMILY BENEFIT

    A percentage of Royalty Funds, collaborations, and assistance will be provided by us to upcoming NFT projects that we, and our community feel that will make a lasting impact on the NFT space.

    We will also be investing in NFT's. Investments in these will be facilitated by us and our holders and a percentage of any returns made will be paid out to Toodlers Family holders.
